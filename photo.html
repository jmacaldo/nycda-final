Photography Portfolio


I have been photographing on and off for many years. Armed with a point and shoot camera as an amateur in 2001, I have slowly developed my skills and sense of aesthetic as a photographer. I generally gravitate more towards the editorial/photojournalism style. I favor the use of natural light, “film like” digital black and white imaging, and the unstaged “decisive moment” feel as pioneered early photojounalists such as Robert Capa.
